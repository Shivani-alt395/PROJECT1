NCC CBRN Awareness

National Cadet Corps

1. What is CBRN?

CBRN stands for Chemical, Biological, Radiological and Nuclear. These are hazardous threats that require special protection, training and preparedness to ensure safety.

2. MOPP Level 0–5

  • Level 0: No protective gear worn.
  • Level 1: Mask carried.
  • Level 2: Overgarment worn.
  • Level 3: Mask and overgarment worn.
  • Level 4: Full protective suit.

3. About CBRN

CBRN preparedness ensures NCC cadets are capable of responding effectively during emergencies involving hazardous materials. It builds discipline, leadership and national service readiness.
