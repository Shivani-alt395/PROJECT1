CBRN Awareness Program

National Cadet Corps

1. What is CBRN?

CBRN stands for Chemical, Biological, Radiological and Nuclear. These are hazardous threats that require special training, discipline and protective measures for safety.

2. MOPP Level 0–5

  • Level 0: No protective gear worn.
  • Level 1: Mask carried.
  • Level 2: Overgarment worn.
  • Level 3: Mask and overgarment worn.
  • Level 4: Full protective suit.

3. About CBRN

CBRN preparedness ensures that NCC cadets are capable of responding effectively during emergencies involving hazardous materials. Proper training enhances safety, leadership and national service readiness.
