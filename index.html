CBRN Awareness

Learn about Chemical, Biological, Radiological & Nuclear hazards

1. What is CBRN?

CBRN stands for Chemical, Biological, Radiological and Nuclear. These represent categories of hazardous materials that can cause severe harm to people, infrastructure, and the environment.

2. MOPP Level 0–5

  • MOPP Level 0: Protective mask carried.
  • MOPP Level 1: Overgarment worn.
  • MOPP Level 2: Overgarment + boots.
  • MOPP Level 3: Mask + overgarment + boots.
  • MOPP Level 4: Full protective suit + gloves + mask.

3. About CBRN

CBRN education helps individuals understand how to recognize, respond to, and protect against chemical, biological, radiological, and nuclear threats. NCC cadets gain readiness through training and safety protocols.
