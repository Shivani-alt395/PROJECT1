THE ACRONYM
C
CHEMICAL

Poisonous vapors, aerosols, or liquids that have toxic effects on people, animals, or plants.

B
BIOLOGICAL

Microorganisms like viruses or bacteria that cause disease or death (e.g., Anthrax, Ebola).

R
RADIOLOGICAL

The use of "dirty bombs" or industrial spills to spread radioactive material over an area.

N
NUCLEAR

A massive explosion causing intense heat, blinding light, blast pressure, and radioactive fallout.
